# Hi, I'm Ingrid! 👩🏻‍🦰

I am a Front-End Developer with a strong foundation in User Experience Design, having completed a two-year Front-End Development program at Noroff School of Technology and Digital Media and a one-year UX Design program at Gokstad Akademiet.

I am passionate about creating accessible, user-centered, and visually engaging digital experiences. Combining technical development skills with a UX mindset allows me to design and build solutions that are both functional and intuitive. I enjoy transforming ideas into responsive, interactive web applications while continuously exploring new technologies, design principles, and best practices.

My strengths lie in bridging the gap between design and development, ensuring that products not only look great but also provide meaningful and seamless user experiences.

Outside of technology, I have a great passion for music, climbing, and outdoor activities. These interests inspire my creativity, problem-solving skills, and attention to detail in both design and development.
